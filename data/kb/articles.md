## Category: Account

### Q: How do I reset my password?
**A:** Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page to receive a reset email.
**Steps:**
1) Go to the login page.
2) Click **Forgot password**.
3) Enter your account email.
4) Open the reset email and set a new password.
**Notes:**
- Check spam/junk folders if you don't receive the email within a few minutes.

### Q: I cannot log in — what should I do?
**A:** Most login issues are caused by wrong credentials or an unverified email.
**Steps:**
1) Confirm you are using the correct email.
2) Try resetting your password.
3) Check if your email is verified in **Account Settings**.
**Notes:**
- If the issue persists, share the exact error message.

### Q: I did not receive the verification email.
**A:** Request a new verification email and check spam/junk.
**Steps:**
1) Go to **Account Settings → Security**.
2) Click **Resend verification email**.
3) Check spam/junk and search for the product name.
**Notes:**
- Some corporate mail servers delay messages.

### Q: How do I change my email address?
**A:** Update your email from account settings and confirm via verification link.
**Steps:**
1) Go to **Account Settings → Profile → Email**.
2) Enter the new email and save.
3) Confirm the new email using the verification link.
**Notes:**
- Your login email becomes the new address after verification.

### Q: How do I enable or disable 2FA?
**A:** Manage 2FA in **Account Settings → Security**.
**Steps:**
1) Open **Account Settings → Security**.
2) Select **Two-Factor Authentication**.
3) Follow the instructions to enable/disable.
**Notes:**
- Keep backup codes in a safe place.

## Category: Billing

### Q: How does billing work?
**A:** Billing is subscription-based and renews automatically at the start of each cycle.
**Notes:**
- You can view your invoices in **Billing → Invoices**.

### Q: My card was charged twice.
**A:** One charge may be a pending authorization.
**Steps:**
1) Check if one of the charges is marked as **Pending**.
2) Wait 3–5 business days for the pending authorization to disappear.
3) If both charges are settled, contact support with invoice/transaction details.
**Notes:**
- Pending authorizations are common and typically auto-reversed.

### Q: How do I update my payment method?
**A:** Add a new payment method from **Billing → Payment Methods**.
**Steps:**
1) Go to **Billing → Payment Methods**.
2) Add a new card.
3) Set it as default if needed.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A:** Refunds are evaluated case-by-case.
**Steps:**
1) Share the invoice number.
2) Explain the reason for the refund request.
**Notes:**
- Providing usage context helps faster resolution.

### Q: Where can I find my invoices?
**A:** Invoices are available in **Billing → Invoices**.
**Steps:**
1) Go to **Billing → Invoices**.
2) Download the invoice PDF.

## Category: Bugs / Technical

### Q: The app is not loading / blank screen.
**A:** Try refresh, cache clear, or incognito mode.
**Steps:**
1) Refresh the page.
2) Try an incognito/private window.
3) Clear browser cache.
4) Try a different browser.
**Notes:**
- Share browser name/version if it continues.

### Q: I got an unexpected error.
**A:** Send the error message and steps to reproduce.
**Steps:**
1) Copy the exact error text.
2) Describe what you clicked before the error.
3) Share screenshots if possible.

### Q: The app is slow.
**A:** Performance can depend on network and region.
**Steps:**
1) Retry on a stable network.
2) Note your region and the time it occurred.
3) Send a screenshot of the issue if available.
